Exchange Policy:

If you decide that your piece isn't quite enough, you have up to 14 days from the date of delivery to exchange your item(s) from the Bag and Belts category. To ensure a successful exchange, please make sure that the product is still sealed and has not been opened or used.

Customers will be responsible for shipping costs (90 EGP) for exchanges if the item does not have any defect, unless you decide to exchange the item(s) because of a manufacturing defect, the shipping cost is on us.

For Scarves & Bandanas & Lingerie & PJs categories, we do not offer an exchange after delivery for health reasons. But you can open the order in front of the courier.

Please make sure to open the package and check it before the courier leaves.

If the item(s) has a defect, you have 2 options:

  1. Return the whole order with the courier before paying.
  2. Or get your whole order and inform us with no more than 48 hours to offer you a free exchange.

Please note that for any defect, exchanges will be made with the same original item.
